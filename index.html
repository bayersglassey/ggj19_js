Controls:

M to mute/unmute background music.
Arrow keys or WASD to move. Touch things to pick them up, Spacebar to drop them.
You are this bee:
Deliver the rain drops to the Seeds!
Once a seed grows big enough, it blooms into a flower.
Bring the flowers back to your home flower to make it grow.
Don't get eaten by spiders!.. if you do, you'll have to reload the page, just like in real life.
