Controls:

M to mute/unmute background music.
Arrow keys or WASD to move.
Run into the rain drops.
Spacebar to drop them!

Seeds suck up rain drops and grow; once big enough, they bloom into flowers.
Bring the flowers back to your home flower to make it grow.
Don't get eaten by spiders!.. if you do, you'll have to refresh your browser, just like in real life.
